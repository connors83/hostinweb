PRP® Powder

PRP® POWDER

Contains freeze-dried microorganisms that, upon contact with the hydrocarbon, begin to digest it, biodegrading it into water and carbon dioxide. Metabolizes hydrocarbons allowing remediation to occur immediately.

PRP® POWDER is easy to use on; open sea, land, swamps, lagoons, ships, etc.
