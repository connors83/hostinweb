Who are we?

Setein LLC specializes in the export of industrial equipment, lubricants, chemicals, and specialized supplies. We are an international company dedicated to the distribution of high-quality products, including the renowned SOCO brand. Our mission is to provide innovative and reliable solutions to companies and institutions throughout Mexico.

We guarantee the timely procurement and delivery of critical components to markets throughout Mexico and the United States, ensuring uninterrupted operations and maximum efficiency.

Exportation Process Timeline

Step-by-step flow of a standard export procedure.

1. Documentation

Prepare export invoice, packing list, and necessary permits such as the certificate of origin.

2. Product Classification

Determine the HS code to correctly classify your product for customs and tariff purposes.

3. Transport & Logistics

Choose the shipping method (air, sea, land) and Incoterms to define responsibilities.

4. Customs Clearance

Ensure compliance with customs laws in both origin and destination countries to avoid delays.
